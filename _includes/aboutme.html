About Me

I am an associate professor at the College of Intelligence and Computing of Tianjin University. Previously, I joined Tianjin University as an Assistant Professor in 2018. I received the B.S. degree and M.Sc. degree from Heilongjiang University in 2011 and 2014, and the Ph.D. degree from Dalian University of Technology under the supervision of Prof.Peihua Li in 2018. My research interests are on computer vision and deep learning, particularly image/video classification, object detection and deep architectures based on high-order statistical modeling and self-attention mechanism.
